Hello! We are Shutter up Photography.

We photograph with the intention of capturing your most important moments in photographs that are light, genuine, and delicate. What began as a one-man team in 2019 has now grown to a strong team of creatives and photographers with one goal in mind: to provide you with heirloom photographs while also providing an enjoyable experience. We're with you every step of the way as your photographer and guide, from your initial inquiry to the months leading up to your wedding and even afterward..

We take pride in the trust we've earned over the years, and we thrive on our brides referring us to their engaged friends. We humbly acknowledge the high regard in which we are held, and we take great care to ensure that such trust is not betrayed. Every couple is unique, and every wedding is a once-in-a-lifetime event for everyone, including us. We arrive at your wedding day prepared with dependable equipment, creative minds, and a desire to deliver the best.
