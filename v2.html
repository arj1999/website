Joe & Alison Wedding 2021

Booking a wedding photographer entails more than just having someone take your photos. Most of the time, it's a matter of trusting an artist to carry out their vision. One of the best things about our couple, Jason and Koo, is that they have complete creative freedom. Although we would always ask if they had any requests, they would always let us decide what we thought was best. Because they trusted our expertise, the shoot went so smoothly that it didn't feel like work at all.

Aside from Joe and Alison, the location they chose appeared to be designed specifically for photoshoots like this. There was a good mix of indoor and outdoor locations, so when it rained, we could continue shooting indoors. It's also the closest location we've found, located right in the heart of Metro Manila, making it one of the most convenient venues for couples.
